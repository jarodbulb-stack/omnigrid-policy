Data Deletion Instructions

If you wish to request deletion of any data associated with your use of Omnigrid Online, you may do so using any of these methods:

Option 1: Manual Removal

You may disconnect your Facebook Pages from our internal tool at any time. Removing the connection automatically deletes stored tokens and page data.

Option 2: Email Request

Send a message to danomandam@yahoo.com with the subject:
"Data Deletion Request"
Include the email or Facebook ID connected to the app.

Option 3: Facebook Settings

You may also revoke the app's access via your Facebook Business Integrations:

Facebook Settings → Business Integrations → Remove “Affiliate Forge / Omnigrid Online App”
